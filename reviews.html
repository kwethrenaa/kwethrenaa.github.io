About the Fan Club

The Wicked Fan Realm is the ultimate gathering place for fans who share a passion for Olivia Rodrigo's enchanting music, her spellbinding artistry, and the magical journey that has captivated us all...

Membership Benefits

  • Exclusive access to member-only events and meet-and-greets.
  • Early access to concert tickets and merchandise.
  • Monthly newsletters with updates on Olivia's music and projects.
  • Participation in fan contests and giveaways.
  • A dedicated online forum to connect with other fans.

Guidelines for Membership

  • expand_moreEmbrace the Magic of Kindness
    Just as Glinda's kindness shines through, we expect all members to treat others with respect. No hate speech or bullying will be tolerated.
  • expand_moreKeep Discussions Focused on Wicked
    Much like Elphaba's journey of self-discovery, we ask that you keep discussions focused on Olivia Rodrigo and her artistry. Let’s embrace the magic of her music!
  • expand_moreAll Fans are Welcome
    Just as the Wizard’s world welcomes all, we welcome fans of all ages! However, members under 13 should have parental consent to join.
